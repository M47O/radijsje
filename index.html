About us

Discover what Radijsje is all about

Radijsje is a farm-to-table culinary experience available from eary spring to late summer in a variety of locations throughout Southern Virginia. We are passionate about infusing classic Northwestern European cuisine with the bold and unique flavors of the Southern United States. We try to create dishes that are both delicious and unexpected, using only the freshest ingredients sourced directly from local farms and suppliers.

Drawing inspiration from the culinary traditions of the Netherlands, our dishes are both timeless and innovative. We believe in honoring the classics while also pushing boundaries and exploring new flavors and techniques. At the same time, we pay homage to our Southern roots by incorporating familiar ingredients such as smoked meats, hot peppers, and bold spices into our dishes.

We are excited to share our passion for food with you and invite you to join us for a memorable dining experience. Whether you're looking for a romantic evening out, a celebration with friends, or just a delicious meal, we are confident that you will love the unique and delicious food we have to offer you.
